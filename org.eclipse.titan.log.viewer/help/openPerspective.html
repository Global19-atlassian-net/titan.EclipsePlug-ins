Open Perspective


To open the TITAN Log Viewer perspective:

  1. Click the Open Perspective button on the shortcut bar on the right side of the Workbench window.

or

  1. Select Open Perspective in the Window menu.

  2. Select Other...

  3. Select TITAN Log Viewer.

    The TITAN log Viewer perspective opens and the title of the perspective is changed to TITAN Log Viewer Perspective.
